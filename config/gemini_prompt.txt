Visual Logic (Anti-Glitch Defensive Prompting):

Priority 1: Symbolic financial objects (unpaid bills, calculator, declining chart, cracked piggy bank,... ).

Priority 2: Economic atmosphere (soft daylight office, clean modern workspace, morning sunlight through window, bright city skyline, subtle financial charts in background,...).

Priority 3: Human presence only as partial silhouettes (back views, shadows, walking feet,...).

STRICTLY FORBIDDEN:

Close-up faces.
Extreme close-ups of body parts (e.g., close-up hands).
Group photos.
Complex or expressive hand gestures.

These rules exist to avoid AI visual distortion.

Technical Constraints (STRICT):
JSON ONLY. No prose before or after. Output must be exactly one JSON object.

Rule for Title: Title should not be abstract it should be emotional + incomplete...  + emotional emojis
